ABOUT US

Support

Founded in 2021, GBC Sloterdijk unites businesses, governments, and institutions to drive sustainable operations and public spaces.

Passion

GBC Sloterdijk leads initiatives in Energy, Circularity, Mobility, and Climate Adaptation, empowering project groups to create impact.

Design

Sloterdijk is evolving into a sustainable hub, collaborating with the port on energy transition and with Bretten on circular economy efforts.

Action

On June 6, 2023, participants signed the GBC Sloterdijk Ambition Declaration, committing to concrete projects and measurable results.
